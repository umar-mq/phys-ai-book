### The Mission
**"Design and deploy an autonomous agent capable of Semantic Exploration and Manipulation."**

You can choose one of two tracks depending on your budget:

**Track A: The Simulation Specialist (Pure Software)**
*   **Requirement**: A powerful PC (RTX GPU).
*   **Task**: Create a highly complex environment in Isaac Sim (e.g., a hospital room). The robot (NurseBot) must find medicine and bring it to the patient bed.
*   **Bonus**: Add "dynamic actors" (people walking) that the robot must avoid.

**Track B: The Hardware Hacker (Sim-to-Real)**
*   **Requirement**: Jetson Orin Nano + Robot Arm/Mobile Base.
*   **Task**: A simpler environment (Tabletop). The robot must sort objects (Red blocks to Red bin, Blue blocks to Blue bin) based on voice commands.
*   **Focus**: Dealing with real-world noise and lighting.

## Advanced Capstone Specifications

**Architecture Pattern**
For the Expert track, we expect a **Hybrid Deliberative-Reactive Architecture**.
*   **Deliberative Layer (The Captain)**: LLM (Llama-3). Runs at 0.1Hz. Decides high-level goals.
*   **Reactive Layer (The Pilot)**: Nav2/MoveIt. Runs at 20-100Hz. Handles obstacle avoidance and visual servoing.
*   **The Bridge**: A Behavior Tree (BehaviorTree.CPP or py_trees) that manages the switching.

**The "Novelty" Requirement**
To get an A+, you must implement one feature not explicitly taught in the tutorials:
*   **Mapping**: 3D Octomap or semantic mapping (labeling the map with "Kitchen", "Lounge").
*   **Learning**: Use Reinforcement Learning (Isaac Gym) for the grasping policy instead of standard MoveIt planning.
*   **Voice**: Bi-directional voice. The robot should speak back: *"I am having trouble finding the apple, can you give me a hint?"* (Text-to-Speech).

### Recommended Tech Stack
*   **Planner**: LangChain (Python) or Nav2 Behavior Trees (C++).
*   **Vision**: NanoLLaVA (Jetson) or GPT-4o (Cloud).
*   **Control**: ROS 2 Control with hardware interfaces.
*   **Sim**: NVIDIA Isaac Sim 2023.1+.

### Troubleshooting the "Valley of Death"
The "Valley of Death" in robotics is the integration phase.
*   *Issue*: "My LLM takes 5 seconds to reply, and my robot crashes in the meantime."
*   *Solution*: Run the LLM in a separate thread (Async). Ensure the Reactive Layer (Collision Avoidance) is *always* active, even while the Brain is thinking. **Safety Monitor** nodes are mandatory.

## Advanced Capstone Specifications

**Architecture Pattern**
Expert level ke liye humein **Hybrid Architecture** chahiye.
*   **Captain (Sochne wala)**: LLM. Ye suste mein chalta hai (Dair se jawab deta hai). "Maqsad: Kitchen jao".
*   **Pilot (Karne wala)**: Nav2. Ye bohat tez chalta hai. "Samne kursi hai, bach kar niklo".
*   **Bridge**: Behavior Tree jo in dono ko milata hai.

**Extra Number (A+)**
Koi ek cheez khud se add karein jo hum ne nahi sikhayi:
*   **Mapping**: Kamray ka naqsha banayein jis par likha ho "Kitchen", "Bedroom".
*   **Learning**: Haath se pakarne ke liye MoveIt ki jagah Reinforcement Learning use karein.
*   **Bolna**: Robot wapis jawab de. *"Mujhe seb nahi mil raha, kya aap bata sakte hain kahan hai?"* (Text-to-Speech).

### Maslon Ka Hal (Valley of Death)
Robotics mein sab se mushkil marhala "Integration" hai (Sab ko jorna).
*   *Masla*: "Mera LLM sochne mein 5 second laga raha hai, itni dair mein robot deewar mein lag gaya."
*   *Hal*: LLM ko alag thread mein chalayein. "Pilot" (Safety system) ko hamesha on rakhein. Agar Dimagh soch raha hai, tab bhi Aankhein khuli honi chahiyen taake takkar na ho.
